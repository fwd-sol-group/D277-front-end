About Sandpoint

Nestled in the northern part of the state, Sandpoint is a vibrant city with a population of approximately 9,961 residents. Established as a city in 1898, Sandpoint boasts a rich history. Its classification as both urban and suburban underscores its dynamic nature. Sandpoint's average income level is known to vary, but it can often be comparable to state averages, reflecting its attractiveness as a recreational destination and a growing economic center.

Quick Facts about Sandpoint

  1. Sandpoint is located on the shores of Lake Pend Oreille, one of the largest freshwater lakes in the United States, providing ample opportunities for boating and water recreation.
  2. Nearby Schweitzer Mountain Resort offers winter sports enthusiasts a range of activities, making Sandpoint a popular destination for skiing and snowboarding.
  3. The city hosts the annual Festival at Sandpoint, a renowned music festival, and boasts a thriving arts community, including galleries, theaters, and local crafts.
  4. Sandpoint is known for its warm and welcoming community, making it a desirable place to live and visit for those seeking a close-knit environment.
